The sinking of the Titanic

Titanic sank on in April of 1912. She was know as an "unsinkable ship".


What took place the evening Titanic sank?

  • Going 22 knots, the ship sideswipes an iceberg
  • The first 5 compartments fill with water
  • The bow is pulled down
  • The pressure from the bow being under the water cause ship to snap in half
  • The stern float vertically, then also sinks

Facts about the RMS Titanic:

  • Largest ocean liner in service at the time
  • 4 days into her maiden voyage from Southampton to New York
  • An estimated 2,224 people on board
  • Hit an iceberg at 23:40 on April 14, 1912
  • Sank on April 15, 1912
